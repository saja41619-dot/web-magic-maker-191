## Personal Freelance Website — Dark & Modern

A sleek multi-page personal site to showcase your services and let clients reach you. Built with placeholder content you can edit anytime.

### Pages & Routes
- **Home (`/`)** — Hero with your name, role tagline, short pitch, and CTAs ("View Work" / "Hire Me"). Brief highlights of services, featured projects, and a closing contact strip.
- **About (`/about`)** — Bio, profile photo placeholder, what you do, your approach/philosophy, fun facts.
- **Work (`/work`)** — Skills grid (categorized) plus a project portfolio gallery with project cards (image, title, description, tags, link).
- **Resume (`/resume`)** — Work experience timeline, education, certifications, and a "Download CV" button.
- **Contact (`/contact`)** — Contact form (name, email, message), email/phone, and social links (LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter, Instagram — placeholders).

### Design Direction
- **Dark & modern theme**: deep near-black background, subtle gradient accents, soft glow on interactive elements.
- **Accent color**: vibrant electric blue/violet for highlights, buttons, and links.
- **Typography**: clean modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter) with bold display weight for hero/headings.
- **Layout**: generous spacing, large hero typography, card-based sections, smooth hover transitions.
- **Animations**: subtle fade-in / slide-up on scroll for sections.
- Fully responsive for mobile, tablet, and desktop.

### Shared UI
- **Sticky header**: logo/name on the left, nav links (Home, About, Work, Resume, Contact) on the right, mobile hamburger menu.
- **Footer**: copyright, social icons, quick links.

### Content
- All text uses clear placeholders ("Your Name", "Your Role", "Project Title", "Lorem-style bio") so you can swap in real details easily later.
- Contact form is UI-only for now (no backend); can be wired up later if you want submissions saved or emailed.
